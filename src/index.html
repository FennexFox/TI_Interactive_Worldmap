This map uses relationType=Claim, projectUnlockName, nation1, and region1 from TIBilateralTemplate.json. Research is shown as granting claims, which are prerequisites for merger, conquest, or unification possibilities, rather than as performing those actions immediately. Claims without projectUnlockName are included as baseline claims.
